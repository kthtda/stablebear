71     * as multisets (order independent). Endpoints are compared with a numerical
72     * tolerance so that barcodes computed via different (but mathematically
73     * equivalent) routes — e.g. from a point cloud versus a precomputed distance
74     * matrix — still compare equal despite floating-point rounding. Two endpoints
75     * `a` and `b` are considered equal when `|a - b| <= atol + rtol * |b|`, with
76     * infinite endpoints matched exactly. Pass `atol = rtol = 0` for a bitwise

97      // avoiding the per-endpoint tolerance branches below.
